# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: November 18, 2025**

This privacy policy applies to the AniDex app for mobile devices. AniDex is created as a Free service and is intended for use "AS IS".

AniDex does not directly collect, process, or sell any personal information or data from its users.

## Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Please note that AniDex utilizes third-party services. Any data that is collected through its use of third-party services are anonymized to improve the app for its users.

Each third-party service comes with their own Privacy Policy about handling data. Below are the links to the Privacy Policy of the third-party service providers used by the app:

### Mixpanel
Mixpanel provides product analytics. They collect anonymized usage data to help improve the app experience. For more information, visit [Mixpanel's Privacy Policy](https://mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy/).

### RevenueCat
RevenueCat manages in-app subscriptions and purchases. They collect subscription and transaction data to enable subscription management. For more information, visit [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/).

### Apple App Store
All payments are processed through Apple's App Store. We never receive your payment information. Apple's privacy practices are described in [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### AniList
AniDex connects to AniList.co to provide anime and manga tracking functionality. When you authorize the app to access your AniList account, the app can read and update your lists. AniDex does not store your AniList data on any serversâ€”all interactions go directly between the app and AniList.co. For more information about AniList's data practices, visit [AniList's Privacy Policy](https://anilist.co/terms).

#### AniList Account Data (via AniList API)
- AniList account information that you authorize the app to access
- Your AniList username, avatar, and profile information
- Your anime and manga lists and watch/read history
- Updates you make to your lists through the app

When you log in to AniList through AniDex, you authenticate directly with AniList.co. AniDex receives an access token that allows it to access your AniList account on your behalf. Your AniList login credentials (email and password) are never collected by the app.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- The App does not collect your AniList login credentials (email and password)
- The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number beyond what is in your AniList profile
- The App does not track your precise location
- The App does not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
- The App does not collect your Apple ID or payment information (any payments are handled securely by Apple)
- Your AniList access token is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server other than AniList.co

### How We Use Your Information

The third-party services use collected information for:

- **App Improvement**: Understanding how users interact with the App to improve features and fix bugs
- **Performance Monitoring**: Identifying and resolving technical issues and crashes
- **Subscription Management**: Managing in-app purchases and subscription status (if applicable)
- **AniList Integration**: Displaying your anime and manga lists, updating your watch/read progress, and syncing your data with AniList.co

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

### Consent

By using AniDex, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
